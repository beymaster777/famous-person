Louis B Mayer is most known for being the co-owner and the head of Metro Goldwyn Mayer (MGM) studios. He is also known for being a film producer ( a film producer is the person who gets the idea of making that movie and a film director ( a director is a person that tells the actors what the film producer wants to do and how to do it) of MGM studios.

Child Hood

Louis B Mayer was born on July 12, 1884 in the Russian Empire. In his childhood he moved to Saint John, New Brunswick to escape the Russian Empire's cruel treatment of the jewish people.He dropped out of school at the age of 12 because of money problems. He worked at a scrap iron to get money for his family. In 1904 Louis B Mayer moved to boston.

Family

He was married to Marget Shenberg from 1904 to 1957.He had 2 kids, Idith Mayer August 17 1905 and Irene Mayer Senzick April 2 1907. He had another wife named Loryana Layson 1948- 1957.

MGM studios from the start to the end

Louis B Mayer started of his now MGM studios as a small nickelodeon which is a theater which you pay with a nickel in 1907. Louis B Mayer turned his small nickelodeon into the largest chain in New England. ( New England consists of the states Connecticut, Maine,Massachusetts, Vermont, Rhode Island, and New Hampshire). He moved to Los Angeles to expand his business and to increase the supply of pictures in 1917. He changed his studio into two different companies Louis B Mayer Productions And Metro Pictures in 1918. In 1924 he merged Metro Pictures and Louis B Mayer Productions with Goldwyn Pictures which changed the name to Metro Goldwyn mayer (MGM) studios.

Life Awards

Louis B Mayer got the DGA honorary life award and Dga stands for the Director guild of America. The DGA honorary life award is one of the highest awards in the Director Guild of America. Louis B Mayer also got the Academy Honorary which for Louis B Mayer is the Ocars but for other people it could be a different award.

Death

Louis B Mayer died on October 19, 1957. The only thing named after Louis B Mayer is his studio Metro Goldwyn Mayer (MGM)
